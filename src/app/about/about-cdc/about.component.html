Mission

Sustain a quality education that addresses the diverse career goals of local and foreign students, by empowering them with professional work information and competencies as well as behavioral attributes that should prepare them to perform excellently in the workplace and to assume responsibly a respectable peaceful community life anywhere in the country and in the world.

Vision

A Center of Development for professionals in medical allied courses imbued with adequate technological information, professional competencies and behavioral attributes that would make them excel in the workplace and model a meaningful community life.

Objectives

To achieve the school Vision and Mission, Calamba Doctors' College commits to provide students with maximum learning assistance for them to be able to live up to the following key expectations:

  1. Optimized the quality time provided for instruction, benefit from the guidance of instructors who competently turn the teaching-learning process into a rich opportunity for sourcing knowledge and experiencing developmental skills;
  2. Acquire and adopt excellent learning strategies and study patterns/habits that would ensure the mastery of skills and competencies required to complete a chosen course;
  3. Practice newly acquired skills in relevant situations maximizing the use of school instructional facilities and services, and appreciate these through appropriate use and care;
  4. Respect and observe school guidelines, rules and regulations that are meant to conserve, preserve and promote the socio-cultural ideals of the school;
  5. Create initiatives for accessing possible learning pathways that should make certain the attainment of high levels of learning and achievement;
  6. Work for excellence, take pride in being a foundation/scholarship grantee and be a part of the school's vision and mission;
  7. Exemplify the values of love and respect for people's beliefs,cultures,ideals,hardwork and industry and make these vehicles in the attainment of a profession that could sustain an appreciative living.
